Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Important Notice

This website and the privacy policy are a mockup for illustriative purposes only. Sunblock is not an app that has been released and this privacy policy is not intended to be binding until finalized.

Your Privacy Matters

At Sunblock, we believe your data is yours. Period.

What We Collect

We process all the information needed for the app to function on your device. No information is sent to the Sunblock developer

How We Use Your Data

All app blocking, daylight tracking, and streak counting happens locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

Third-Party Services

Sunblock does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. To use Sunblock you need to grant the app permission to read your Apple Health time in daylight data and grant screen time permission. Apple Health and Screen Time are subject to Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Apple Watch Integration

Daylight time is verified using your Apple Watch's ambient light sensor. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with Sunblock or anyone else.

Data Deletion

Your data is not sent off of your device or associated with any accounts. If you would like to delete your data from Sunblock, you can uninstall the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy policy, we'll update this page.
